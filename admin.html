🎥 Sunday Video Announcement

Upload one weekly announcement video to Cloudinary and schedule it for Sundays only. During the configured Sunday time window, the video plays full-screen over the two-panel grid. Outside the schedule, the regular flyers and programs display as usual.

After this date/time, the video is no longer shown on display.

Recommended: MP4, 720p, 1-2 minutes, and 100MB or less.
